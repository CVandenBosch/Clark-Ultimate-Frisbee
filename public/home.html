We are the Woo Town Killer Bees!

We LOVE Frisbee! We compete in D3 club league tournaments and jump into fun tournaments against other schools whenever we can.

Join Us!

Play competitive, fun Ultimate! Go to tournaments in the great outdoors! Meet cool people! Throw discs. We are a small but mighty team that welcomes new faces. Come join us, no experience necessary!

Spirit of the Game

We aim to promote knowledge, love of Ultimate Frisbee, and the Spirit of the Game. We stress the importance of sportsmanship, fair play, teamwork, and the desire to better oneself as an Ultimate player and as a human being.
